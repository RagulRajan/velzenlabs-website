Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

VelzenLabs respects your right to control your personal data. If you want to delete the data we have collected through your use of our services or your Meta (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp) integration, you can follow the steps below.

How to Request Data Deletion

1
Send us an email Email privacy@velzenlabs.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include your name and the email address or Meta account associated with our services.
2
We verify your identity To protect your privacy, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request. This helps ensure that no one else can delete your data on your behalf.
3
We process your request Once verified, we will delete your personal data from our systems within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when the deletion is complete.

Removing Meta Permissions

You can also revoke VelzenLabs' access to your Meta data directly from your Meta account:

1
Facebook Go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites, find VelzenLabs, and click Remove.
2
Instagram Go to Settings → Security → Apps and Websites, find VelzenLabs, and click Remove.
When you revoke our access through Meta or request deletion through email, we will delete all Meta Platform Data associated with your account from our systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained if required by law.

What Data Gets Deleted

Upon a valid deletion request, we will delete:

  • Your personal information (name, email, contact details)
  • Meta account data (Facebook Page, Instagram, WhatsApp information)
  • Messages and conversation logs processed by our AI services
  • Usage logs and technical data linked to your account

Data that has been anonymized or aggregated in a way that can no longer identify you may be retained for analytics and service improvement purposes.
