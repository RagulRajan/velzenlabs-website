About Velzen Labs

We build AI that works

Velzen Labs is an AI automation company based in Tamil Nadu. We help small and mid-size businesses — especially jewellery retailers and hotels — eliminate repetitive manual work and run smarter using AI.

Our Mission

Make AI automation accessible to every Indian business.

AI automation has been a privilege of large enterprises with big IT budgets. We're changing that. A jeweller in Coimbatore or a hotel in Madurai should have the same tools as a Fortune 500 company — at a price that makes sense for their business.

We build workflows that run in the background — handling customer messages, tracking inventory, generating reports, following up on leads — so your team can focus on what actually needs a human touch.

Why We Started

We saw great businesses lose customers to slow responses.

We kept seeing the same problem: a customer messages a jewellery shop on WhatsApp asking about a gold ring. Nobody replies for 4 hours because the owner is at the counter, the staff are busy, and nobody's job it is to handle digital enquiries.

That customer buys from a competitor who replied in 10 minutes — not because their product is better, but because they were faster.

We built Velzen Labs to fix that. Not just for one shop — but for every jeweller, every hotel, every growing Indian business that deserves a system that works as hard as they do.
